# His Weekly Plan: 4-Day Powerlifting Foundation

## Program Overview

**Duration:** 1 Week (repeatable with progressive overload)
**Frequency:** 4 days per week
**Focus:** Build strength foundation in squat, bench press, and deadlift
**Equipment:** Half rack, adjustable bench, barbell, dumbbells
**Rest Days:** Wednesday, Saturday, Sunday

---

## Weekly Schedule

| Mon | Tue | Wed | Thu | Fri | Sat | Sun |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| Squat Focus | Bench Focus | REST | Deadlift Focus | Volume Day | REST | REST |

---

## Day 1 - Monday: Squat Focus

### Warm-Up (15 min)
- Jumping jacks: 2x20
- Bodyweight squats: 2x10
- Hip circles: 10 each direction
- Leg swings: 10 each direction per leg
- Bar work up: Empty bar x10 → 135x5 → 185x3 → 225x2 → Working weight x1

### Main Workout

| Exercise | Sets x Reps | RPE | Rest |
|----------|-------------|-----|------|
| Back Squat | 5x5 | 7-8 | 3-4 min |
| Pause Squat | 3x3 @ 80% | 7-8 | 2-3 min |
| Romanian Deadlift | 4x8-10 | 7 | 2 min |
| Overhead Press | 3x8 | 7-8 | 2 min |
| Bulgarian Split Squat | 3x10 each | - | 90 sec |
| Plank Hold | 3x45-60 sec | - | 60 sec |

**Squat Cues:**
- Feet shoulder-width, toes out 15-30°
- Big breath, brace core hard
- Break at hips and knees together
- Hip crease below knee (full depth)
- Drive through mid-foot

---

## Day 2 - Tuesday: Bench Press Focus

### Warm-Up (12 min)
- Arm circles: 20 each direction
- Band pull-aparts: 2x15
- Push-ups: 2x10
- Bar work up: Empty bar x10 → 95x5 → 135x3 → 155x2 → Working weight x1

### Main Workout

| Exercise | Sets x Reps | RPE | Rest |
|----------|-------------|-----|------|
| Bench Press | 5x5 | 7-8 | 3-4 min |
| Close-Grip Bench | 4x6 @ 80% | 7-8 | 2-3 min |
| Barbell Row | 4x8 | 7-8 | 2 min |
| Incline DB Press | 3x10-12 | - | 90 sec |
| Single-Arm DB Row | 3x12 each | - | 90 sec |
| Skull Crushers | 3x10-12 | - | 90 sec |
| Barbell Curl | 3x10-12 | - | 60 sec |

**Bench Cues:**
- Retract and depress scapula
- Arch upper back, glutes on bench
- Bar to lower chest, elbows 45°
- Press with slight arc toward face
- Full lockout every rep

---

## Day 3 - Wednesday: REST

Active recovery optional:
- Light walking (20-30 min)
- Foam rolling
- Stretching

---

## Day 4 - Thursday: Deadlift Focus

### Warm-Up (15 min)
- Light cardio: 3-5 min
- Cat-cow: 2x10
- Glute bridges: 2x10
- Bird dogs: 2x8 each side
- Bar work up: 135x5 → 185x3 → 225x2 → 275x1 → Working weight x1

### Main Workout

| Exercise | Sets x Reps | RPE | Rest |
|----------|-------------|-----|------|
| Conventional Deadlift | 5x3 | 8-9 | 4-5 min |
| Pause Deadlift | 3x2 @ 80% | 8 | 3-4 min |
| Front Squat | 3x6 | 7 | 2-3 min |
| DB Romanian Deadlift | 3x12-15 | - | 90 sec |
| Walking Lunges | 3x8 each | - | 2 min |
| Ab Wheel or Plank | 3x10-15 | - | 60 sec |

**Deadlift Cues:**
- Bar over mid-foot
- Hips and shoulders rise together
- Engage lats ("break the bar")
- Push floor away, don't yank
- Full lockout: hips through, stand tall

---

## Day 5 - Friday: Volume/Technique Day

### Warm-Up (10 min)
- Full body dynamic warm-up
- Work up to 60-70% on each lift

### Main Workout

| Exercise | Sets x Reps | Intensity | Rest |
|----------|-------------|-----------|------|
| Tempo Squat (3-1-0) | 4x5 | 70% | 2-3 min |
| Paused Bench (2 sec) | 4x5 | 70% | 2-3 min |
| Deficit/Conv Deadlift | 4x3 | 70% | 3 min |
| Flat DB Bench | 3x12 | - | 90 sec |
| DB Romanian Deadlift | 3x15 | - | 90 sec |
| Barbell Row | 3x10 | - | 2 min |
| DB Overhead Press | 3x10 | - | 90 sec |

**Optional Core Circuit:**
- Plank: 60 sec
- Side plank: 30 sec each
- Dead bugs: 15 each side

---

## Progressive Overload

### Week-to-Week
- Lower body: Add 5-10 lbs when completing all sets at target RPE
- Upper body: Add 2.5-5 lbs when completing all sets at target RPE

### Deload (Every 4-6 weeks)
- Reduce weight to 60-70% of working weights
- Keep sets/reps same or reduce by 50%
