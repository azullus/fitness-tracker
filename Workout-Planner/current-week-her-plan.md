# 5-Day Cardio, Mobility & Light Strength Program

## Program Overview

**Duration:** 1 Week (repeatable)
**Frequency:** 5 days per week
**Focus:** HIIT cardio, mobility, core strength, light toning
**Equipment:** Dumbbells, resistance bands (optional), yoga mat
**Rest Days:** Saturday and Sunday

---

## Weekly Split

| Day | Focus | Duration |
|-----|-------|----------|
| Monday | HIIT + Core | 35-45 min |
| Tuesday | Lower Body + Mobility | 40-50 min |
| Wednesday | Cardio Circuit | 30-40 min |
| Thursday | Upper Body + Core | 35-45 min |
| Friday | Yoga/Active Recovery | 45-60 min |
| Sat/Sun | REST | - |

---

## Day 1 - Monday: HIIT + Core

### Warm-Up (5 minutes)
- March in place: 1 minute
- Arm circles: 30 seconds each direction
- Hip circles: 30 seconds each direction
- Jumping jacks: 1 minute
- Bodyweight squats: 10 reps

### HIIT Circuit (20 minutes)
**Perform each exercise for 40 seconds, rest 20 seconds. Complete 3 rounds.**

**Round 1-3:**
1. High knees
2. Squat jumps (or regular squats for low impact)
3. Mountain climbers
4. Burpees (step-out modification available)
5. Skaters (lateral jumps)

*Rest 1 minute between rounds*

### Core Finisher (10 minutes)
**Perform each exercise for 45 seconds, 15 seconds rest**

1. Dead bugs
2. Bicycle crunches
3. Plank hold
4. Russian twists (bodyweight or light dumbbell)
5. Leg raises
6. Bird dogs (alternating)

### Cool-Down (5 minutes)
- Child's pose: 1 minute
- Cat-cow: 1 minute
- Lying spinal twist: 1 minute each side
- Deep breathing: 1 minute

---

## Day 2 - Tuesday: Lower Body + Mobility

### Dynamic Warm-Up (8 minutes)
- Leg swings (front/back): 15 each leg
- Leg swings (side to side): 15 each leg
- Walking lunges: 10 each leg
- Glute bridges: 15 reps
- Fire hydrants: 10 each leg
- Ankle circles: 10 each direction per ankle

### Lower Body Strength (25 minutes)

**1. Goblet Squat (Dumbbell)**
- Sets/Reps: 3x12
- Rest: 60 seconds
- Focus: Hold dumbbell at chest, sit back into squat

**2. Romanian Deadlift (Dumbbell)**
- Sets/Reps: 3x12
- Rest: 60 seconds
- Focus: Slight knee bend, push hips back, feel hamstring stretch

**3. Walking Lunges (Bodyweight or Light Dumbbells)**
- Sets/Reps: 3x10 each leg
- Rest: 60 seconds
- Focus: Knee tracks over ankle, controlled movement

**4. Glute Bridge (Single Leg)**
- Sets/Reps: 3x10 each leg
- Rest: 45 seconds
- Focus: Squeeze glute at top, don't let hips drop

**5. Calf Raises**
- Sets/Reps: 3x15
- Rest: 45 seconds
- Focus: Full range of motion, pause at top

**6. Clamshells (with band if available)**
- Sets/Reps: 3x15 each side
- Rest: 45 seconds
- Focus: Keep hips stacked, control the movement

### Mobility Flow (12 minutes)

Hold each position for 45-60 seconds:
1. **90/90 Hip Stretch** - each side
2. **Pigeon Pose** - each side
3. **Frog Stretch** - center
4. **Figure-4 Stretch** - each side
5. **Couch Stretch or Hip Flexor Lunge** - each side
6. **Seated Forward Fold**

---

## Day 3 - Wednesday: Cardio Circuit

### Warm-Up (5 minutes)
- Jump rope or jumping jacks: 2 minutes
- Arm swings: 1 minute
- Bodyweight squats: 15 reps
- High knees: 1 minute

### Cardio Circuit (25-30 minutes)
**5 Stations, 45 seconds work / 15 seconds transition. Complete 4 rounds.**

**Station 1: Box Step-Ups (or Stair Steps)**
- Alternate legs, maintain steady pace
- Add light dumbbells for challenge

**Station 2: Jumping Jacks (or Power Jacks)**
- Keep core engaged
- Land softly

**Station 3: Shadow Boxing**
- Jab, cross, hook, uppercut
- Stay light on feet, engage core

**Station 4: Speed Skaters**
- Side-to-side lateral jumps
- Touch floor on each landing (optional)

**Station 5: Squat to Front Kick**
- Squat down, alternate front kicks at top
- Engage core for balance

*Rest 1 minute between rounds*

### AMRAP Finisher (5 minutes)
As Many Rounds As Possible:
- 10 Burpees (or squat + step back)
- 20 Mountain climbers (10 each leg)
- 30 Jumping jacks

### Cool-Down (5 minutes)
- Walk in place: 1 minute
- Standing quad stretch: 30 seconds each
- Standing hamstring stretch: 30 seconds each
- Shoulder stretch: 30 seconds each
- Deep breathing: 1 minute

---

## Day 4 - Thursday: Upper Body + Core

### Warm-Up (5 minutes)
- Arm circles: 1 minute (30 sec each direction)
- Shoulder rolls: 30 seconds
- Cat-cow: 1 minute
- Push-up to downward dog: 5 reps
- Band pull-aparts or arm swings: 1 minute

### Upper Body Strength (25 minutes)

**1. Dumbbell Chest Press**
- Sets/Reps: 3x12
- Rest: 60 seconds
- Focus: Lower with control, press up explosively

**2. Bent-Over Dumbbell Row**
- Sets/Reps: 3x12 each arm
- Rest: 60 seconds
- Focus: Squeeze shoulder blade at top, elbow close to body

**3. Dumbbell Shoulder Press**
- Sets/Reps: 3x10
- Rest: 60 seconds
- Focus: Press straight up, core engaged

**4. Dumbbell Bicep Curls**
- Sets/Reps: 3x12
- Rest: 45 seconds
- Focus: Control the negative, no swinging

**5. Tricep Dips (Bench or Chair)**
- Sets/Reps: 3x12
- Rest: 45 seconds
- Focus: Elbows point backward, lower to 90 degrees

**6. Lateral Raises**
- Sets/Reps: 3x12
- Rest: 45 seconds
- Focus: Light weight, control movement, slight bend in elbows

### Core Circuit (10 minutes)
**2 rounds, 30 seconds each exercise, 10 seconds rest**

1. Plank with shoulder taps
2. Side plank (left)
3. Side plank (right)
4. V-ups or tuck crunches
5. Reverse crunches
6. Dead bug hold
7. Superman hold
8. Hollow body hold

### Cool-Down (5 minutes)
- Chest doorway stretch: 30 seconds each side
- Tricep stretch: 30 seconds each arm
- Cross-body shoulder stretch: 30 seconds each
- Child's pose with arms extended: 1 minute
- Deep breathing: 1 minute

---

## Day 5 - Friday: Yoga/Active Recovery

### Gentle Flow Sequence (45-60 minutes)

**Opening (5 minutes)**
- Seated meditation and breath work
- Cat-cow stretches: 2 minutes
- Thread the needle: 1 minute each side

**Sun Salutation Flow (10 minutes)**
Complete 4-5 slow sun salutations:
1. Mountain pose (Tadasana)
2. Arms overhead (Urdhva Hastasana)
3. Forward fold (Uttanasana)
4. Half lift (Ardha Uttanasana)
5. Step back to plank
6. Lower to floor or chaturanga
7. Cobra or upward dog
8. Downward dog (hold 5 breaths)
9. Step forward to half lift
10. Forward fold
11. Rise to mountain

**Standing Balance & Strength (15 minutes)**
Hold each pose for 5-8 breaths:
- Warrior I (each side)
- Warrior II (each side)
- Extended side angle (each side)
- Triangle pose (each side)
- Tree pose (each side)
- Chair pose
- Eagle pose (each side)

**Hip Opening Sequence (10 minutes)**
Hold each for 1-2 minutes:
- Low lunge with twist (each side)
- Lizard pose (each side)
- Pigeon pose (each side)
- Happy baby

**Floor Stretches & Relaxation (10 minutes)**
Hold each for 1-2 minutes:
- Seated forward fold
- Butterfly pose
- Reclined twist (each side)
- Legs up the wall (or legs elevated)

**Savasana (5 minutes)**
- Final relaxation
- Body scan meditation
- Deep breathing

## Workout Modifications

### Low Impact Options
- **Burpees:** Squat + step back to plank + step forward
- **Jumping jacks:** Step-out jacks
- **High knees:** Marching in place
- **Squat jumps:** Regular squats with calf raise
- **Mountain climbers:** Slow, controlled stepping

### Intensity Increases
- Add ankle weights for lower body work
- Use heavier dumbbells
- Reduce rest periods by 10-15 seconds
- Add extra round to circuits
- Increase work intervals to 50 seconds

## Recovery Tips

1. **Hydration:** Drink water before, during, and after workouts
2. **Foam Rolling:** 5-10 minutes on rest days
3. **Sleep:** Aim for 7-8 hours
4. **Nutrition:** Protein within 30-60 min post-workout
5. **Listen to Your Body:** Take extra rest if needed

---

## When to Progress

After 3-4 weeks, consider:
- Adding 5 minutes to cardio sessions
- Increasing dumbbell weights by 2-5 lbs
- Adding a 4th round to circuits
- Reducing rest periods
- Adding resistance bands to lower body work
