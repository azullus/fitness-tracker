## STORAGE & FOOD SAFETY

- Rotisserie chicken: Use within 3-4 days or freeze portions
- Ground meats: Use within 1-2 days or freeze immediately
- Pork tenderloin: Cook within 3-4 days or freeze
- Beef stew: Stores well 4-5 days in fridge
- Egg muffins: Store 5-7 days refrigerated or freeze

---

## SUBSTITUTION OPTIONS

**Dietary Restrictions:**
- Gluten-free: Use GF bread, tortillas, and crackers
- Dairy-free: Use plant-based milk, yogurt, and cheese alternatives
- Low-carb: Reduce rice/grains, increase vegetables
- Vegetarian: Substitute tofu, tempeh, or legumes for meats

**Budget Alternatives:**
- Use whole chicken instead of parts (break down yourself)
- Frozen vegetables when fresh prices are high
- Dried beans instead of canned (prep in batch)
- Store brand products (PC, No Name) save 20-30%
