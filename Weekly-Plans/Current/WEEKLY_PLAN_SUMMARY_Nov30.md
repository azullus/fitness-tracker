## Customization Options

### Dietary Modifications

**Gluten-Free:**
- Use GF bread, tortillas, flour
- Verify soy sauce is GF (use tamari)
- Replace regular oats with certified GF oats

**Dairy-Free:**
- Coconut or almond yogurt
- Plant-based milk (oat, almond, soy)
- Nutritional yeast instead of cheese

**Low-Carb/Keto:**
- Replace rice with cauliflower rice
- Skip tortillas, use lettuce wraps
- Reduce root vegetables, increase greens
- Add extra healthy fats (avocado, nuts, oils)

**Vegetarian:**
- Replace meats with tofu, tempeh, or seitan
- Add more legumes (lentils, chickpeas)
- Increase eggs and dairy for protein

## Food Safety Notes

### Refrigerator Storage Times
- Raw ground meat: 1-2 days
- Raw chicken: 1-2 days
- Raw pork chops/tenderloin: 3-4 days
- Cooked leftovers: 3-4 days
- Hard-boiled eggs: 1 week
- Egg muffins: 5-7 days

### Freezer Storage (0°F)
- Ground meat: 3-4 months
- Chicken parts: 9 months
- Pork chops: 4-6 months
- Cooked stew: 2-3 months
- Bread: 3 months

### Thawing Guidelines
- Refrigerator: 24 hours per 5 lbs
- Cold water: 30 min per lb (in sealed bag)
- Never thaw on counter
- Cook immediately after thawing

## Emergency Substitutions

If items are out of stock:

**Proteins:**
- Ground beef ↔ Ground turkey
- Pork tenderloin ↔ Pork chops
- Chicken thighs ↔ Drumsticks or breasts
- Rotisserie chicken ↔ Cooked chicken breast

**Vegetables:**
- Broccoli ↔ Cauliflower or Brussels sprouts
- Sweet potato ↔ Regular potato or squash
- Bell peppers ↔ Zucchini
- Spinach ↔ Kale or Swiss chard

**Grains:**
- Quinoa ↔ Brown rice or farro
- Brown rice ↔ Wild rice or barley
- Whole wheat bread ↔ Multigrain or sourdough
