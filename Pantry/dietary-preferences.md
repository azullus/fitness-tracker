# Dietary & Food Preferences

Reference file for meal planning agents. Always check this before generating meal plans.

---

## Allergies & Restrictions

| Person | Allergy | Notes |
|--------|---------|-------|
| **Him** | Bananas | Cannot eat raw bananas. CAN eat banana bread/muffins (cooked banana is fine). Substitute with **yellow kiwi** or other fruit. |
| Her | None | No food allergies |

---

## Staple Preferences

### Grains & Bread

| Item | Preference | Notes |
|------|------------|-------|
| **Rice** | WHITE ONLY | Never use brown rice. Always white rice. |
| **Bread** | Whole grain | Flexible on brand - choose cheapest quality option |

### Nut Butters

| Instead of... | Use... |
|---------------|--------|
| Peanut butter | **PB Fit** (powdered peanut butter) |
| Almond butter | **PB Fit** |

*Note: Adjust liquid ratios in recipes since PB Fit is mixed with water.*

## Snack Preferences

### Protein Bars (Primary)
- **BUILT Marshmallow Bars** (Costco) - first choice
- BUILT PUFF bars - alternative

### Savory Snacks (Rotate These)
- Pepperoni sticks
- Beef jerky
- Pickled eggs

### Other Snacks (Mix In)
- Apple + PB Fit
- Greek yogurt
- Cheese + crackers
- Hummus + veggies
- Trail mix

**Snack Strategy**: Don't default to the same snacks every day. Rotate between protein bars, savory options, and fresh alternatives for variety.

## Shopping Notes

### Store Priority
1. **Costco** - Bulk proteins, BUILT bars, pantry staples
2. **Safeway** - Sale meats, fresh produce
3. **Superstore** - Budget items, cheapest bread options

### Budget
- Weekly target: $150-200 CAD for 2 people
- Always check sales flyers before planning

## Quick Reference for Meal Planning

**Always:**
- Use white rice (never brown)
- Substitute yellow kiwi for raw bananas (for Him)
- Use PB Fit instead of regular nut butters
- Include high-protein snacks
- Vary snack options throughout the week

**Never:**
- Raw bananas in His meals
- Brown rice
- Regular peanut/almond butter
