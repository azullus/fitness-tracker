# Pepperoni Cheese Bites

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 5 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 320 kcal |
| Protein | 22g |
| Carbs | 4g |
| Fat | 26g |
| Fiber | 0g |
| Sodium | 1100mg |

## Ingredients

- 50g pepperoni slices
- 60g cheddar cheese, cubed
- 60g mozzarella cheese, cubed
- 10 whole grain crackers
- 2 tbsp Dijon mustard (optional)
- Fresh cracked pepper

## Instructions

1. Arrange pepperoni slices on a plate
2. Add cheese cubes alongside
3. Fan out crackers
4. Add small dish of Dijon for dipping
5. Season cheese with fresh cracked pepper
6. Serve immediately or pack for on-the-go

## Notes

- Storage: Pack components separately for work/gym
- Substitutions: Beef jerky, salami, or turkey pepperoni
- Meal Prep: Pre-portion into containers for week
