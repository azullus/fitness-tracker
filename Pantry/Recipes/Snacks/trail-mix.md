# Homemade Trail Mix

**Servings:** 4 (make batch, portion for week)
**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 5 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving - 1/4 cup)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 240 kcal |
| Protein | 7g |
| Carbs | 24g |
| Fat | 14g |
| Fiber | 3g |

## Ingredients

- 1 cup mixed nuts (almonds, cashews, walnuts)
- 1/2 cup dried cranberries
- 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips
- 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds (optional)
- 1/4 cup coconut flakes (optional)

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl
2. Mix well to distribute evenly
3. Divide into 4 portions (about 1/4 cup each)
4. Store in small containers or snack bags

## Notes

- **Storage:** Keeps 2-3 weeks in airtight container at room temperature
- **Meal Prep:** Make large batch on Sunday, portion for the week
- **Substitutions:** Use any dried fruit (raisins, apricots, mango)
- **Banana Allergy:** Avoid banana chips; use other dried fruits for her
- **Lower Sugar:** Skip chocolate chips, add more nuts
