# Cottage Cheese Cucumber Bites

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 10 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 180 kcal |
| Protein | 18g |
| Carbs | 10g |
| Fat | 8g |
| Fiber | 2g |
| Sodium | 420mg |

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cottage cheese
- 1 large English cucumber
- 2 tbsp everything bagel seasoning
- Fresh dill, chopped
- Fresh cracked pepper
- Optional: smoked salmon pieces for extra protein

## Instructions

1. Slice cucumber into 1/2 inch thick rounds
2. Arrange on plate or platter
3. Top each round with spoonful of cottage cheese
4. Sprinkle with everything bagel seasoning
5. Add fresh dill and cracked pepper
6. Optional: add small piece of smoked salmon on top
7. Serve immediately

## Notes

- Storage: Best eaten fresh, cucumber gets watery
- Substitutions: Greek yogurt instead of cottage cheese
- Meal Prep: Not recommended
