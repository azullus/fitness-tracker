# Quick Pickled Eggs

**Servings:** 6 eggs (3 per person)
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 12 minutes
**Total Time:** 27 minutes + pickling time

## Nutrition Facts (per serving - 3 eggs)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 210 kcal |
| Protein | 18g |
| Carbs | 3g |
| Fat | 15g |
| Fiber | 0g |
| Sodium | 480mg |

## Ingredients

- 6 large eggs
- 1 cup white vinegar
- 1/2 cup water
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp whole peppercorns
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 bay leaf
- Optional: 1 beet for color, sliced jalapeno for heat

## Instructions

1. Hard boil eggs: cover with cold water, bring to boil, cover and remove from heat, let sit 12 minutes
2. Transfer to ice bath, peel when cool
3. Combine vinegar, water, sugar, salt in small pot
4. Bring to boil, stirring to dissolve sugar
5. Add peppercorns, garlic, bay leaf
6. Place eggs in jar, pour hot brine over
7. Let cool, refrigerate
8. Best after 24-48 hours pickling

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 3-4 weeks refrigerated
- Substitutions: Apple cider vinegar for different flavor
- Meal Prep: Make a batch, snack all week
