# No-Bake Protein Energy Balls

**Servings:** 12 balls (6 per person)
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 15 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving - 3 balls)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 280 kcal |
| Protein | 18g |
| Carbs | 28g |
| Fat | 10g |
| Fiber | 4g |
| Sodium | 120mg |

## Ingredients

- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1/2 cup PB Fit, mixed with 3 tbsp water
- 1/4 cup honey
- 1 scoop vanilla or chocolate protein powder
- 2 tbsp mini chocolate chips
- 2 tbsp chia seeds
- 1/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions

1. Mix PB Fit with water until smooth paste forms
2. In large bowl, combine oats, protein powder, chia seeds, salt
3. Add PB Fit mixture and honey
4. Mix until well combined
5. Fold in chocolate chips and coconut
6. Refrigerate 10 minutes if too sticky
7. Roll into 12 balls using about 2 tbsp each
8. Store in airtight container in refrigerator

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 1 week refrigerated, 3 months frozen
- Substitutions: Any protein powder flavor, dried fruit instead of chips
- Meal Prep: Perfect batch prep snack
