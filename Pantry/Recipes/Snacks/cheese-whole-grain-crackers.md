# Cheese & Whole Grain Crackers

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 5 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 260 kcal |
| Protein | 12g |
| Carbs | 24g |
| Fat | 14g |
| Fiber | 3g |

## Ingredients

- 4 oz aged cheddar cheese, cubed or sliced
- 20 whole grain crackers
- 1 medium apple, sliced (optional)
- Fresh grapes (optional)

## Instructions

1. Cut cheese into small cubes or slices
2. Arrange crackers on a plate
3. Add cheese cubes to plate
4. Add sliced apple or grapes if desired
5. Serve as an easy snack plate

## Notes

- **Storage:** Prep cheese cubes ahead; keeps 1 week refrigerated
- **Substitutions:** Use any hard cheese (gouda, swiss, pepper jack)
- **Crackers:** Triscuits, Wheat Thins, or Mary's Gone Crackers
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
- **Higher Protein:** Add sliced deli turkey or nuts
