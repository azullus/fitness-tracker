# Beef Jerky & Cheese Protein Plate

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 5 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 340 kcal |
| Protein | 32g |
| Carbs | 12g |
| Fat | 18g |
| Fiber | 2g |
| Sodium | 980mg |

## Ingredients

- 60g beef jerky
- 60g aged cheddar, sliced
- 1 apple, sliced
- 2 tbsp PB Fit mixed with 1 tbsp water
- 10 almonds
- 1 tbsp honey for drizzling (optional)

## Instructions

1. Arrange beef jerky on one side of plate
2. Fan out cheddar slices
3. Add apple slices
4. Place PB Fit dip in small dish
5. Scatter almonds
6. Drizzle honey over cheese if desired
7. Serve immediately or pack for portable snack

## Notes

- Storage: Pack separately for work/gym snack
- Substitutions: Turkey jerky, different cheese varieties
- Meal Prep: Pre-portion jerky and cheese for quick grab
