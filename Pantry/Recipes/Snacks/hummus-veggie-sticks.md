# Hummus & Veggie Sticks

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 5 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 180 kcal |
| Protein | 8g |
| Carbs | 22g |
| Fat | 7g |
| Fiber | 7g |

## Ingredients

- 1 cup hummus (store-bought or homemade)
- 2 cups baby carrots
- 2 cups celery sticks
- 1 cup bell pepper strips (any color)

## Instructions

1. Divide hummus between two small bowls or containers
2. Wash and prep vegetables
3. Cut celery into 3-4 inch sticks
4. Slice bell peppers into strips
5. Arrange vegetables around hummus
6. Serve immediately or pack for grab-and-go

## Notes

- **Storage:** Keeps 3-4 days refrigerated; keep hummus separate from veggies
- **Meal Prep:** Prep veggie sticks on Sunday for the week
- **Substitutions:** Add cucumber, cherry tomatoes, or snap peas
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
- **Extra Protein:** Use black bean hummus or add chickpeas
