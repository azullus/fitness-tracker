# Greek Yogurt with Honey & Almonds

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 2 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 2 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 200 kcal |
| Protein | 18g |
| Carbs | 24g |
| Fat | 5g |
| Fiber | 1g |

## Ingredients

- 2 cups plain Greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp honey
- 2 tbsp sliced almonds

## Instructions

1. Divide Greek yogurt between two bowls
2. Drizzle 1 tbsp honey over each
3. Sprinkle 1 tbsp sliced almonds on each
4. Serve immediately

## Notes

- **Storage:** Best eaten fresh; yogurt keeps 1 week in fridge
- **Substitutions:** Use maple syrup instead of honey
- **Extra Toppings:** Add chia seeds, berries, or granola
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
- **Higher Protein:** Use Icelandic skyr instead of Greek yogurt
