# Sausage & Egg Breakfast Hash

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 18 minutes
**Total Time:** 28 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 520 kcal |
| Protein | 38g |
| Carbs | 28g |
| Fat | 30g |
| Fiber | 4g |
| Sodium | 880mg |

## Ingredients

- 4 breakfast sausage links, sliced into coins
- 4 large eggs
- 2 cups frozen hash browns (or diced potatoes)
- 1 bell pepper, diced
- 1/2 onion, diced
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh chives for garnish

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high
2. Add hash browns, season with paprika, salt, pepper
3. Cook 8-10 minutes until golden, stirring occasionally
4. Add sausage, peppers, onions, cook 5 minutes
5. Create 4 wells in the hash
6. Crack eggs into wells
7. Cover, cook 3-4 minutes until eggs set
8. Top with cheese, cover 1 minute to melt
9. Garnish with chives

## Notes

- Storage: Best eaten fresh
- Substitutions: Turkey sausage, egg whites only
- Meal Prep: Prep veggies ahead
