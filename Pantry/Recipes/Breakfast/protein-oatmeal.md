# High-Protein Oatmeal Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Cook Time:** 10 minutes
**Total Time:** 15 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 450 kcal |
| Protein | 36g |
| Carbs | 52g |
| Fat | 12g |
| Fiber | 8g |
| Sodium | 320mg |

## Ingredients

- 1 cup rolled oats
- 2 cups milk (or water)
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- 2 tbsp PB Fit, mixed with 1 tbsp water
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
- Mixed berries for topping
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- Pinch of cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Bring milk to a simmer in pot
2. Add oats and cinnamon, cook 5 minutes stirring occasionally
3. Remove from heat, let cool 2 minutes
4. Stir in protein powder until smooth
5. Divide between two bowls
6. Top with Greek yogurt, PB Fit drizzle, honey
7. Add berries and chia seeds

## Notes

- Storage: Oatmeal keeps 3 days, add toppings fresh
- Substitutions: Almond milk, any protein powder flavor
- Meal Prep: Make oats ahead, reheat with splash of milk
