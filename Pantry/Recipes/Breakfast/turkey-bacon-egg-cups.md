# Turkey Bacon Egg Cups

**Servings:** 2 (3 cups each)
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 18 minutes
**Total Time:** 28 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 380 kcal |
| Protein | 34g |
| Carbs | 4g |
| Fat | 26g |
| Fiber | 1g |
| Sodium | 820mg |

## Ingredients

- 6 slices turkey bacon
- 6 large eggs
- 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
- 1/4 cup bell pepper, finely diced
- 2 tbsp milk
- Fresh chives, chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Cooking spray

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
2. Spray 6 muffin cups with cooking spray
3. Line each cup with a slice of turkey bacon around edges
4. Whisk eggs with milk, salt, pepper
5. Stir in bell pepper and half the cheese
6. Pour egg mixture into bacon-lined cups
7. Top with remaining cheese
8. Bake 15-18 minutes until eggs are set
9. Let cool 3 minutes, run knife around edges to release
10. Garnish with chives

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 5 days refrigerated, reheat in microwave
- Substitutions: Regular bacon, spinach, mushrooms
- Meal Prep: Perfect make-ahead breakfast
