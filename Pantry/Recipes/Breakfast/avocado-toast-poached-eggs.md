# Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 8 minutes
**Total Time:** 18 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 420 kcal |
| Protein | 20g |
| Carbs | 38g |
| Fat | 22g |
| Fiber | 10g |

## Ingredients

- 4 slices whole grain bread
- 2 ripe avocados
- 4 large eggs
- 1 tbsp white vinegar (for poaching)
- Cherry tomatoes, halved (for garnish)
- Red pepper flakes
- Salt, pepper
- Lemon juice (1/2 lemon)

## Instructions

1. Toast bread slices until golden
2. Cut avocados in half, remove pit, scoop flesh into bowl
3. Mash avocado with fork, add lemon juice, salt, and pepper
4. Bring pot of water to gentle simmer, add vinegar
5. Crack each egg into small cup
6. Create gentle whirlpool in water, slide eggs in one at a time
7. Poach 3-4 minutes for runny yolk (longer for firmer)
8. Remove eggs with slotted spoon, drain on paper towel
9. Spread mashed avocado on toast
10. Top each toast with poached egg
11. Garnish with tomatoes, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper

## Notes

- **Storage:** Must be eaten immediately; does not store well
- **Substitutions:** Fried or scrambled eggs work if poaching is difficult
- **Extra Protein:** Add smoked salmon or crumbled feta
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
