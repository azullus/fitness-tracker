# Protein Smoothie Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 10 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 410 kcal |
| Protein | 35g |
| Carbs | 52g |
| Fat | 8g |
| Fiber | 7g |

## Ingredients

### Base
- 2 cups frozen mixed berries
- 2 scoops vanilla protein powder
- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 cup milk

### Toppings
- 1/4 cup granola
- 2 tbsp chia seeds
- 2 tbsp coconut flakes
- Fresh fruit (see notes)

## Instructions

1. Add frozen berries, protein powder, Greek yogurt, and milk to blender
2. Blend until thick and smooth (should be thicker than a smoothie)
3. If too thin, add more frozen fruit; if too thick, add splash of milk
4. Pour into two bowls
5. Arrange toppings in rows on top
6. Serve immediately

## Notes

- **Storage:** Must be eaten immediately; does not store well
- **Banana Allergy - IMPORTANT:**
  - For HIM: Can add sliced banana as topping
  - For HER: Use berries, mango, or peaches instead
- **Substitutions:** Any frozen fruit works (mango, peaches, cherries)
- **Extra Protein:** Use high-protein milk or add PB Fit powder
