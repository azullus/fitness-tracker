# Cottage Cheese Fruit Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 8 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 8 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 380 kcal |
| Protein | 32g |
| Carbs | 42g |
| Fat | 10g |
| Fiber | 5g |
| Sodium | 580mg |

## Ingredients

- 2 cups cottage cheese (2%)
- 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
- 1 yellow kiwi, sliced (use instead of banana)
- 2 tbsp honey or maple syrup
- 2 tbsp PB Fit, mixed with 1 tbsp water
- 2 tbsp granola
- 1 tbsp chia seeds

## Instructions

1. Divide cottage cheese between two bowls
2. Arrange mixed berries and kiwi slices on top
3. Mix PB Fit with water until smooth, drizzle over bowls
4. Add honey or maple syrup
5. Sprinkle with granola and chia seeds
6. Serve immediately

## Notes

- Storage: Best eaten fresh
- Substitutions: Any seasonal fruit (no raw bananas for Him)
- Meal Prep: Prep toppings ahead, assemble fresh
