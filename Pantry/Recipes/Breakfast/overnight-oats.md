# Overnight Oats with Chia Seeds

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes (overnight refrigeration)
**Total Time:** 5 minutes + overnight

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 385 kcal |
| Protein | 14g |
| Carbs | 68g |
| Fat | 8g |
| Fiber | 9g |

## Ingredients

- 1 cup rolled oats
- 2 cups milk (dairy or plant-based)
- 2 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- Fruit topping (see notes for options)

## Instructions

1. Divide oats between two jars or containers
2. Add 1 cup milk to each container
3. Add 1 tbsp chia seeds to each
4. Add 1/2 tsp cinnamon and 1 tbsp maple syrup to each
5. Stir well to combine
6. Cover and refrigerate overnight (minimum 4 hours)
7. In the morning, stir and add desired toppings
8. Eat cold or microwave for 1-2 minutes if preferred warm

## Notes

- **Storage:** Keeps 3-4 days in refrigerator
- **Banana Allergy - IMPORTANT:**
  - For HIM: Add sliced banana as topping
  - For HER: Use berries, sliced peaches, or apple chunks instead
- **Substitutions:** Use any milk alternative (oat, almond, soy)
- **Meal Prep:** Make 4-5 jars on Sunday for the whole week
