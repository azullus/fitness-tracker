# Veggie Scramble with Whole Wheat Toast

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 8 minutes
**Total Time:** 18 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 395 kcal |
| Protein | 26g |
| Carbs | 32g |
| Fat | 18g |
| Fiber | 5g |

## Ingredients

- 6 large eggs
- 1 cup diced bell peppers
- 1 cup fresh spinach
- 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
- 1/4 cup shredded cheese
- 4 slices whole wheat toast
- 2 tsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium heat
2. Add bell peppers and mushrooms, saut√© for 3-4 minutes until softened
3. Add spinach and cook until wilted, about 1 minute
4. Whisk eggs in a bowl with salt and pepper
5. Pour eggs over vegetables, stirring gently as they cook
6. When eggs are almost set, sprinkle cheese on top
7. Remove from heat (residual heat will finish cooking)
8. Serve immediately with whole wheat toast

## Notes

- **Storage:** Best eaten fresh; eggs don't reheat well
- **Substitutions:** Use any vegetables on hand (zucchini, tomatoes, onions)
- **Add Protein:** Crumble in cooked turkey sausage for extra protein
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
