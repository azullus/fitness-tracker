# Greek Yogurt Parfait

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 5 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 320 kcal |
| Protein | 20g |
| Carbs | 42g |
| Fat | 8g |
| Fiber | 4g |

## Ingredients

- 2 cups plain Greek yogurt (1 cup per person)
- 1/2 cup granola
- 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries)
- 2 tbsp honey
- 2 tbsp chia seeds

## Instructions

1. Divide Greek yogurt between two bowls or jars
2. Layer granola over yogurt
3. Top with mixed berries
4. Drizzle with honey
5. Sprinkle chia seeds on top
6. Serve immediately or refrigerate overnight for meal prep

## Notes

- **Storage:** Can be prepped night before; add granola in morning to keep it crunchy
- **Substitutions:** Use maple syrup instead of honey if preferred
- **Meal Prep:** Layer in mason jars for grab-and-go breakfast
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
