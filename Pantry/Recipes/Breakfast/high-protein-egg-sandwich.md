# High-Protein Egg Sandwich

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Cook Time:** 10 minutes
**Total Time:** 15 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 480 kcal |
| Protein | 38g |
| Carbs | 32g |
| Fat | 22g |
| Fiber | 4g |
| Sodium | 820mg |

## Ingredients

- 4 large eggs
- 4 slices whole grain bread
- 4 slices Canadian bacon
- 2 slices cheddar cheese
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 avocado, sliced
- Hot sauce (optional)
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Toast bread to desired crispness
2. Heat small amount of butter in skillet
3. Fry eggs to your preference (over medium recommended)
4. In same pan, warm Canadian bacon 1 minute per side
5. Assemble: bread, cheese, Canadian bacon, eggs, avocado
6. Season with salt, pepper, hot sauce if desired
7. Top with second slice of bread

## Notes

- Storage: Best eaten fresh
- Substitutions: Turkey bacon or regular bacon
- Meal Prep: Not recommended
