# Whole Grain Pancakes with Berries

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 15 minutes
**Total Time:** 25 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 425 kcal |
| Protein | 16g |
| Carbs | 72g |
| Fat | 9g |
| Fiber | 6g |

## Ingredients

- 1.5 cups whole wheat flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 eggs
- 1.5 cups milk
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup fresh berries
- 4 tbsp pure maple syrup (for serving)

## Instructions

1. Whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl
2. In separate bowl, whisk eggs, milk, honey, and vanilla
3. Pour wet ingredients into dry, stir until just combined (lumps are okay)
4. Heat non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat
5. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake
6. Cook until bubbles form on surface, about 2 minutes
7. Flip and cook another 1-2 minutes until golden
8. Serve with fresh berries and maple syrup

## Notes

- **Storage:** Freeze extra pancakes between parchment paper; reheat in toaster
- **Substitutions:** Use buttermilk for fluffier pancakes
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
- **Add Protein:** Mix 1 scoop protein powder into batter
