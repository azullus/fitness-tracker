# Turkey Meatball Sub

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 18 minutes
**Total Time:** 28 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 560 kcal |
| Protein | 48g |
| Carbs | 45g |
| Fat | 20g |
| Fiber | 5g |
| Sodium | 980mg |

## Ingredients

- 450g ground turkey
- 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
- 1 egg
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1 cup marinara sauce
- 2 whole grain sub rolls
- 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
- Fresh basil leaves
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C)
2. Mix turkey, breadcrumbs, egg, garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper
3. Form into 12 meatballs, place on baking sheet
4. Bake 15-18 minutes until cooked through
5. Heat marinara in small pot
6. Slice sub rolls, add meatballs
7. Spoon marinara over meatballs, top with mozzarella
8. Broil 1-2 minutes to melt cheese, garnish with basil

## Notes

- Storage: Meatballs keep 4 days, reheat and assemble fresh
- Substitutions: Lean ground beef or chicken
- Meal Prep: Batch cook meatballs, freeze up to 3 months
