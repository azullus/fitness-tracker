# Tuna White Rice Power Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 15 minutes
**Total Time:** 25 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 520 kcal |
| Protein | 45g |
| Carbs | 52g |
| Fat | 12g |
| Fiber | 4g |
| Sodium | 580mg |

## Ingredients

- 2 cans tuna in water, drained (240g)
- 1.5 cups white rice, cooked
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 1 cup edamame, shelled
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp sesame seeds
- 2 green onions, sliced

## Instructions

1. Cook white rice according to package directions
2. While rice cooks, drain tuna and flake into a bowl
3. Mix soy sauce, sesame oil, and rice vinegar for dressing
4. Divide rice between two bowls
5. Top with tuna, edamame, and avocado slices
6. Drizzle with dressing and garnish with sesame seeds and green onions

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 2 days refrigerated (add avocado fresh)
- Substitutions: Canned salmon works great too
- Meal Prep: Cook rice in bulk, assemble bowls day-of
