# Steak Salad with Blue Cheese

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 12 minutes
**Total Time:** 22 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 540 kcal |
| Protein | 52g |
| Carbs | 14g |
| Fat | 32g |
| Fiber | 4g |
| Sodium | 680mg |

## Ingredients

- 400g sirloin steak
- 4 cups mixed greens
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1/2 red onion, sliced
- 60g blue cheese, crumbled
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Season steak generously with salt and pepper
2. Heat cast iron skillet over high heat
3. Sear steak 4-5 minutes per side for medium (adjust to preference)
4. Rest steak 5 minutes, then slice against the grain
5. Whisk olive oil, balsamic, and mustard for dressing
6. Arrange greens, tomatoes, and onion in bowls
7. Top with sliced steak and blue cheese
8. Drizzle with dressing and serve

## Notes

- Storage: Best eaten fresh, steak gets tough reheated
- Substitutions: Goat cheese or feta instead of blue cheese
- Meal Prep: Cook steak ahead, serve cold on salad
