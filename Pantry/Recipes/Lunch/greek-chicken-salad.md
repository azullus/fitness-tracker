# Greek Chicken Salad

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 15 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 495 kcal |
| Protein | 48g |
| Carbs | 18g |
| Fat | 26g |
| Fiber | 5g |
| Sodium | 720mg |

## Ingredients

- 350g rotisserie chicken, shredded
- 4 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
- 1 cucumber, diced
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 100g feta cheese, crumbled
- 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup kalamata olives
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes, onion, and olives in a large bowl
2. Whisk olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper for dressing
3. Add shredded chicken to salad
4. Top with crumbled feta
5. Drizzle with dressing and toss gently
6. Serve immediately

## Notes

- Storage: Keep dressing separate, assembled salad keeps 1 day
- Substitutions: Grilled chicken breast or canned chicken
- Meal Prep: Prep veggies ahead, assemble when ready to eat
