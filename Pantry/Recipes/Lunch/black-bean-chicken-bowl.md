# Southwest Chicken Black Bean Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 15 minutes
**Total Time:** 25 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 580 kcal |
| Protein | 52g |
| Carbs | 55g |
| Fat | 16g |
| Fiber | 12g |
| Sodium | 720mg |

## Ingredients

- 350g chicken breast, diced
- 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1.5 cups white rice, cooked
- 1 cup corn kernels
- 1/2 cup salsa
- 1 avocado, diced
- 2 tbsp taco seasoning
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/4 cup Greek yogurt (sour cream substitute)
- Fresh cilantro
- Lime wedges

## Instructions

1. Cook white rice according to package directions
2. Season chicken with taco seasoning
3. Heat oil in skillet over medium-high
4. Cook chicken 6-8 minutes until golden and cooked through
5. Add black beans and corn, heat through (2 min)
6. Divide rice between bowls
7. Top with chicken mixture, salsa, avocado
8. Add Greek yogurt, cilantro, and lime wedges

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 4 days refrigerated without avocado
- Substitutions: Ground beef or turkey work great
- Meal Prep: Excellent batch cooking recipe
