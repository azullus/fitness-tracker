# Turkey Avocado Protein Wrap

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 10 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 485 kcal |
| Protein | 42g |
| Carbs | 35g |
| Fat | 20g |
| Fiber | 8g |
| Sodium | 890mg |

## Ingredients

- 300g deli turkey breast, sliced
- 2 large whole grain tortillas
- 1 avocado, mashed
- 4 slices Swiss cheese
- 1 cup spinach
- 1 tomato, sliced
- 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Lay tortillas flat and spread mashed avocado evenly
2. Layer turkey slices down the center
3. Add Swiss cheese, spinach, and tomato slices
4. Drizzle with Dijon mustard
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Roll tightly, tucking in sides as you go
7. Slice in half diagonally to serve

## Notes

- Storage: Wrap tightly in foil, keeps 1 day refrigerated
- Substitutions: Chicken breast or ham work well
- Meal Prep: Prep ingredients, assemble morning-of
