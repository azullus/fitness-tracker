# Ham & Cheese Croissant Melt

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Cook Time:** 8 minutes
**Total Time:** 13 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 520 kcal |
| Protein | 34g |
| Carbs | 32g |
| Fat | 30g |
| Fiber | 2g |
| Sodium | 1100mg |

## Ingredients

- 2 large croissants
- 200g deli ham, sliced
- 4 slices Swiss cheese
- 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp butter, softened
- Mixed greens for serving

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
2. Slice croissants in half horizontally
3. Spread Dijon mustard on bottom halves
4. Layer ham and Swiss cheese
5. Replace top halves
6. Brush tops with softened butter
7. Bake 6-8 minutes until cheese melts and croissant is golden
8. Serve with mixed greens

## Notes

- Storage: Best eaten fresh, can reheat in oven
- Substitutions: Turkey, chicken, or roast beef
- Meal Prep: Not recommended, croissants go stale
