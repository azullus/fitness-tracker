# Korean Beef Lettuce Wraps

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 12 minutes
**Total Time:** 22 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 520 kcal |
| Protein | 45g |
| Carbs | 18g |
| Fat | 28g |
| Fiber | 3g |
| Sodium | 780mg |

## Ingredients

- 450g lean ground beef
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 head butter lettuce
- 2 green onions, sliced
- 1 tbsp sesame seeds
- Sriracha (optional)

## Instructions

1. Brown ground beef in large skillet, drain excess fat
2. Add garlic and ginger, cook 1 minute
3. Mix soy sauce, brown sugar, and sesame oil
4. Pour sauce over beef, simmer 3-4 minutes until slightly thickened
5. Separate butter lettuce into cups
6. Spoon beef mixture into lettuce cups
7. Top with green onions, sesame seeds, and sriracha if desired

## Notes

- Storage: Beef keeps 4 days refrigerated, assemble cups fresh
- Substitutions: Ground turkey or chicken work well
- Meal Prep: Cook beef ahead, reheat and assemble
