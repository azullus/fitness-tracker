# Cottage Cheese Veggie Power Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 10 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 380 kcal |
| Protein | 36g |
| Carbs | 28g |
| Fat | 14g |
| Fiber | 6g |
| Sodium | 620mg |

## Ingredients

- 2 cups cottage cheese (2% or 4%)
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 cucumber, diced
- 1 avocado, cubed
- 2 tbsp everything bagel seasoning
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Fresh cracked pepper
- Optional: 4 slices whole grain toast

## Instructions

1. Divide cottage cheese between two bowls
2. Arrange tomatoes, cucumber, and avocado around cottage cheese
3. Drizzle with olive oil
4. Sprinkle generously with everything bagel seasoning
5. Add fresh cracked pepper
6. Serve with whole grain toast if desired

## Notes

- Storage: Best eaten immediately, avocado browns quickly
- Substitutions: Greek yogurt for different texture
- Meal Prep: Prep veggies ahead, assemble when ready
