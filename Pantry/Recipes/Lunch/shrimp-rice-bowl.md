# Garlic Shrimp Rice Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 15 minutes
**Total Time:** 25 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 480 kcal |
| Protein | 42g |
| Carbs | 48g |
| Fat | 12g |
| Fiber | 3g |
| Sodium | 680mg |

## Ingredients

- 400g large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1.5 cups white rice, cooked
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon, juiced
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Red pepper flakes (optional)

## Instructions

1. Cook white rice according to package directions
2. Season shrimp with salt and pepper
3. Heat olive oil and 1 tbsp butter in large skillet over medium-high
4. Add shrimp, cook 2 minutes per side until pink, remove
5. Add remaining butter and garlic, saut√© 30 seconds
6. Return shrimp, add lemon juice and parsley
7. Serve shrimp over rice with cherry tomatoes

## Notes

- Storage: Best eaten fresh, shrimp gets rubbery when reheated
- Substitutions: Chicken breast cut into strips
- Meal Prep: Prep rice ahead, cook shrimp fresh
