# Smoked Salmon Avocado Toast

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 3 minutes
**Total Time:** 13 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 480 kcal |
| Protein | 32g |
| Carbs | 32g |
| Fat | 26g |
| Fiber | 8g |
| Sodium | 920mg |

## Ingredients

- 200g smoked salmon
- 4 slices whole grain bread
- 2 ripe avocados
- 4 tbsp cream cheese
- 2 tbsp capers
- 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
- Fresh dill for garnish
- Lemon wedges
- Everything bagel seasoning
- Fresh cracked pepper

## Instructions

1. Toast bread until golden and crispy
2. Spread cream cheese evenly on each slice
3. Mash avocado and spread over cream cheese
4. Layer smoked salmon on top
5. Add capers and red onion slices
6. Sprinkle with everything bagel seasoning
7. Garnish with fresh dill and serve with lemon wedges

## Notes

- Storage: Best eaten immediately
- Substitutions: Canned salmon (drained) for budget option
- Meal Prep: Not recommended, assemble fresh
