# Deli Meat & Cheese Roll-Ups

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 10 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 450 kcal |
| Protein | 44g |
| Carbs | 8g |
| Fat | 28g |
| Fiber | 2g |
| Sodium | 1200mg |

## Ingredients

- 200g deli turkey breast
- 100g deli ham
- 6 slices provolone cheese
- 4 dill pickle spears
- 2 tbsp cream cheese
- 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 cup baby spinach
- Toothpicks for securing

## Instructions

1. Lay out turkey and ham slices flat
2. Spread thin layer of cream cheese on each slice
3. Add a line of Dijon mustard
4. Layer provolone cheese and spinach
5. Place pickle spear at one end
6. Roll tightly around the pickle
7. Secure with toothpicks, slice in half to serve

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 2-3 days refrigerated wrapped in plastic
- Substitutions: Any deli meat and cheese combo works
- Meal Prep: Perfect make-ahead lunch, keeps well
