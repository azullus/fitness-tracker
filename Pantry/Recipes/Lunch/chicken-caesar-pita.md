# Chicken Caesar Stuffed Pita

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 12 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 12 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 510 kcal |
| Protein | 48g |
| Carbs | 38g |
| Fat | 18g |
| Fiber | 4g |
| Sodium | 840mg |

## Ingredients

- 350g rotisserie chicken, shredded
- 2 whole wheat pita pockets
- 2 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
- 1/4 cup parmesan cheese, shaved
- 4 tbsp Caesar dressing
- 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, quartered
- 1/4 cup croutons, crushed
- Fresh cracked pepper

## Instructions

1. Cut pitas in half to create pockets
2. In a bowl, toss chicken with Caesar dressing
3. Add romaine, tomatoes, and half the parmesan
4. Toss to combine
5. Stuff mixture into pita pockets
6. Top with remaining parmesan and crushed croutons
7. Season with pepper and serve

## Notes

- Storage: Pita gets soggy, eat within a few hours
- Substitutions: Canned chicken or grilled chicken breast
- Meal Prep: Keep components separate until serving
