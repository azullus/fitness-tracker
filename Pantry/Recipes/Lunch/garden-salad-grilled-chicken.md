# Garden Salad with Grilled Chicken

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 12 minutes
**Total Time:** 27 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 380 kcal |
| Protein | 42g |
| Carbs | 18g |
| Fat | 16g |
| Fiber | 5g |

## Ingredients

### Chicken
- 1 lb chicken breast
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt, pepper, garlic powder
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning

### Salad
- 4 cups mixed greens
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 cucumber, sliced
- 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup sunflower seeds
- 4 tbsp balsamic vinaigrette

## Instructions

1. Pound chicken breasts to even thickness (about 3/4 inch)
2. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and Italian seasoning
3. Heat olive oil in grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat
4. Cook chicken 5-6 minutes per side until internal temp reaches 165Â°F
5. Let rest 5 minutes, then slice
6. Divide mixed greens between two large bowls
7. Top with tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion
8. Arrange sliced chicken on top
9. Sprinkle with sunflower seeds
10. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette

## Notes

- **Storage:** Keep dressing separate; salad keeps 1-2 days refrigerated
- **Substitutions:** Use pre-cooked rotisserie chicken to save time
- **Extra Protein:** Add hard-boiled eggs or chickpeas
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
