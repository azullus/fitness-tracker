# High-Protein Egg Salad Lettuce Cups

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 12 minutes
**Total Time:** 27 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 420 kcal |
| Protein | 32g |
| Carbs | 8g |
| Fat | 30g |
| Fiber | 3g |
| Sodium | 520mg |

## Ingredients

- 8 large eggs
- 3 tbsp Greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 2 stalks celery, finely diced
- 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
- 1 head butter lettuce
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Paprika for garnish

## Instructions

1. Place eggs in pot, cover with cold water, bring to boil
2. Once boiling, cover and remove from heat, let sit 12 minutes
3. Transfer eggs to ice bath, peel when cool
4. Chop eggs and place in bowl
5. Add Greek yogurt, mustard, celery, and dill
6. Season with salt and pepper, mix gently
7. Separate butter lettuce leaves into cups
8. Spoon egg salad into lettuce cups, sprinkle with paprika

## Notes

- Storage: Egg salad keeps 3 days refrigerated
- Substitutions: Mayo instead of Greek yogurt (higher fat)
- Meal Prep: Make egg salad ahead, assemble cups fresh
