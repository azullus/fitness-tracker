# Loaded Chicken Quesadilla

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 10 minutes
**Total Time:** 20 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 580 kcal |
| Protein | 52g |
| Carbs | 38g |
| Fat | 24g |
| Fiber | 4g |
| Sodium | 920mg |

## Ingredients

- 350g cooked chicken breast, shredded
- 4 large whole grain tortillas
- 1.5 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
- 1 bell pepper, sliced thin
- 1/2 onion, sliced thin
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salsa and Greek yogurt for serving

## Instructions

1. Season chicken with cumin and chili powder
2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in skillet, saut√© peppers and onions until soft (5 min)
3. Remove veggies, wipe pan, add remaining oil
4. Place tortilla in pan, add cheese, chicken, veggies, more cheese, top tortilla
5. Cook until golden (2-3 min per side)
6. Repeat with second quesadilla
7. Cut into wedges, serve with salsa and Greek yogurt

## Notes

- Storage: Refrigerate up to 3 days, reheat in pan to crisp
- Substitutions: Use rotisserie chicken for faster prep
- Meal Prep: Pre-cook chicken and veggies, assemble fresh
