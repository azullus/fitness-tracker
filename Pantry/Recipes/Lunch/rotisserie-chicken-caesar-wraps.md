# Rotisserie Chicken Caesar Wraps

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 10 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 380 kcal |
| Protein | 32g |
| Carbs | 35g |
| Fat | 12g |
| Fiber | 4g |

## Ingredients

- 2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken (from Costco)
- 4 whole wheat tortillas (10-inch)
- 2 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
- 1/4 cup light Caesar dressing
- 2 tbsp parmesan cheese, grated
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- Fresh cracked black pepper

## Instructions

1. Warm tortillas in microwave for 15 seconds (makes them pliable)
2. Lay tortillas flat on work surface
3. Divide lettuce among tortillas, placing in center
4. Top with shredded chicken
5. Add cherry tomatoes
6. Drizzle with Caesar dressing
7. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese and pepper
8. Fold bottom of tortilla up, then fold sides in and roll tightly
9. Cut in half diagonally to serve

## Notes

- **Storage:** Wrap tightly in foil; keeps 1 day refrigerated (dressing may make it soggy)
- **Substitutions:** Use grilled chicken breast if no rotisserie available
- **Meal Prep Tip:** Keep dressing separate until ready to eat
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
