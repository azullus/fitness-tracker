# Chicken & Quinoa Power Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes (if using pre-cooked ingredients)
**Total Time:** 15 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 465 kcal |
| Protein | 35g |
| Carbs | 45g |
| Fat | 16g |
| Fiber | 9g |

## Ingredients

### Bowl Base
- 2 cups cooked chicken (shredded rotisserie or grilled)
- 2 cups cooked quinoa
- 2 cups mixed greens

### Toppings
- 1 cup roasted chickpeas
- 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 2 tbsp sunflower seeds

### Lemon-Tahini Dressing
- 3 tbsp tahini
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2-3 tbsp water (to thin)
- Salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Cook quinoa according to package directions (or use pre-cooked)
2. Make dressing: whisk tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, and water
3. Divide mixed greens between two bowls
4. Add quinoa to each bowl
5. Top with shredded chicken
6. Arrange chickpeas, tomatoes, and avocado on top
7. Drizzle with lemon-tahini dressing
8. Sprinkle with sunflower seeds

## Notes

- **Storage:** Store components separately; assemble when ready to eat
- **Meal Prep:** Cook quinoa and chicken on Sunday for the week
- **Substitutions:** Use any grain (brown rice, farro, bulgur)
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
