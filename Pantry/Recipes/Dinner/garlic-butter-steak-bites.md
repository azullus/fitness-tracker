# Garlic Butter Steak Bites

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 10 minutes
**Total Time:** 20 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 520 kcal |
| Protein | 52g |
| Carbs | 4g |
| Fat | 32g |
| Fiber | 1g |
| Sodium | 480mg |

## Ingredients

- 450g sirloin steak, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 3 tbsp butter
- 5 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- Fresh rosemary, chopped
- Fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Pat steak cubes dry, season with salt, pepper, Italian seasoning
2. Heat oil in cast iron over high heat
3. Add steak in single layer, don't overcrowd
4. Sear 2 minutes per side for medium-rare
5. Add butter and garlic, baste steak for 1 minute
6. Remove from heat, add rosemary
7. Let rest 3 minutes
8. Garnish with parsley and serve

## Notes

- Storage: Best eaten immediately
- Substitutions: Any tender cut works (ribeye, strip)
- Meal Prep: Not recommended, steak best fresh
- Pairs with: Roasted potatoes, steamed broccoli, or salad
