# Pork Tenderloin with Roasted Root Vegetables

**Servings:** 2 (with leftovers)
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 35 minutes
**Total Time:** 50 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 420 kcal |
| Protein | 38g |
| Carbs | 35g |
| Fat | 14g |
| Fiber | 6g |

## Ingredients

### Pork
- 1.5 lb pork tenderloin
- 2 tbsp olive oil (divided)
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp paprika
- Fresh thyme and rosemary
- Salt and pepper

### Vegetables
- 3 large carrots, chopped into 1-inch pieces
- 2 parsnips, chopped into 1-inch pieces
- 1 large sweet potato, cubed
- 1 red onion, cut into wedges

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C)
2. Toss vegetables with 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper
3. Spread vegetables on large sheet pan in single layer
4. Roast vegetables for 15 minutes
5. Meanwhile, pat pork dry with paper towels
6. Season pork with garlic powder, paprika, salt, pepper
7. Heat remaining olive oil in oven-safe skillet over high heat
8. Sear pork on all sides until browned, about 2 minutes per side
9. Add fresh herbs on top of pork
10. Place skillet in oven alongside vegetables
11. Roast 18-22 minutes until pork reaches 145°F internal temp
12. Rest pork 5 minutes before slicing
13. Serve sliced pork over roasted vegetables

## Notes

- **Storage:** Keeps 3-4 days refrigerated; great for meal prep
- **Leftovers:** Use for sandwiches or salads next day
- **Substitutions:** Use any root vegetables in season
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
