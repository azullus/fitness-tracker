# Chicken Fajita Rice Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 12 minutes
**Cook Time:** 15 minutes
**Total Time:** 27 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 560 kcal |
| Protein | 48g |
| Carbs | 52g |
| Fat | 18g |
| Fiber | 6g |
| Sodium | 720mg |

## Ingredients

- 400g chicken breast, sliced thin
- 1.5 cups white rice, cooked
- 2 bell peppers, sliced
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 tbsp fajita seasoning
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lime, juiced
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
- Fresh cilantro
- Salsa for topping

## Instructions

1. Cook white rice according to package directions
2. Season chicken with fajita seasoning
3. Heat 1 tbsp oil in skillet over high heat
4. Cook chicken 5-6 minutes until done, remove
5. Add remaining oil, saut√© peppers and onions 5 minutes
6. Return chicken, add lime juice, toss
7. Divide rice between bowls
8. Top with fajita mixture
9. Add avocado, Greek yogurt, cilantro, and salsa

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 4 days (without avocado)
- Substitutions: Shrimp or steak strips
- Meal Prep: Cook chicken and veggies ahead
