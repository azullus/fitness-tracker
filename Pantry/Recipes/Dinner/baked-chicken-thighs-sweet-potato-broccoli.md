# Baked Chicken Thighs with Sweet Potato & Broccoli

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 40 minutes
**Total Time:** 55 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 465 kcal |
| Protein | 36g |
| Carbs | 38g |
| Fat | 18g |
| Fiber | 7g |

## Ingredients

- 1.5 lb bone-in chicken thighs (4 pieces)
- 2 large sweet potatoes, cubed (1-inch pieces)
- 3 cups broccoli florets
- 3 tbsp olive oil (divided)
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- Salt and pepper
- Fresh lemon for serving

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)
2. Toss sweet potato cubes with 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper
3. Spread sweet potatoes on one half of large sheet pan
4. Pat chicken thighs dry with paper towels
5. Mix paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper
6. Rub chicken with 1 tbsp olive oil, then season generously
7. Place chicken skin-side up on other half of sheet pan
8. Roast for 25 minutes
9. Toss broccoli with remaining olive oil and seasonings
10. Add broccoli to pan around chicken and potatoes
11. Continue roasting 12-15 minutes until chicken reaches 165°F
12. Squeeze fresh lemon over broccoli before serving

## Notes

- **Storage:** Keeps 3-4 days refrigerated
- **Crispy Skin:** Make sure chicken is dry and don't overcrowd pan
- **Substitutions:** Use boneless thighs (reduce cook time by 10 min)
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
