# Shrimp Scampi with Pasta

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 15 minutes
**Total Time:** 25 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 580 kcal |
| Protein | 42g |
| Carbs | 52g |
| Fat | 22g |
| Fiber | 3g |
| Sodium | 720mg |

## Ingredients

- 400g large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 200g linguine or spaghetti
- 4 tbsp butter
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 cup white wine (or chicken broth)
- 1 lemon, juiced
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Parmesan for serving

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta according to package, reserve 1/2 cup pasta water
2. Season shrimp with salt and pepper
3. Heat oil in large skillet, cook shrimp 2 min per side, remove
4. Add butter and garlic, cook 1 minute
5. Add wine, lemon juice, red pepper flakes
6. Simmer 2-3 minutes
7. Add pasta, toss with sauce, add pasta water if needed
8. Return shrimp, add parsley
9. Serve with parmesan

## Notes

- Storage: Best eaten fresh
- Substitutions: Chicken broth instead of wine
- Meal Prep: Not recommended
