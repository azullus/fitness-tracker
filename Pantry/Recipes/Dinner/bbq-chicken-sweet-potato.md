# BBQ Chicken Stuffed Sweet Potatoes

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 20 minutes
**Total Time:** 30 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 520 kcal |
| Protein | 48g |
| Carbs | 52g |
| Fat | 12g |
| Fiber | 7g |
| Sodium | 680mg |

## Ingredients

- 350g rotisserie chicken, shredded
- 2 large sweet potatoes
- 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
- 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 2 tbsp Greek yogurt
- 2 green onions, sliced
- 1 tbsp butter
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Pierce sweet potatoes, microwave 8-10 minutes until tender
2. Or bake at 400Â°F for 45 min (if time allows)
3. Mix shredded chicken with BBQ sauce in small pot, warm through
4. Split sweet potatoes lengthwise
5. Fluff insides with fork, add butter, salt, pepper
6. Top with BBQ chicken
7. Add cheddar cheese
8. Microwave 30 seconds to melt cheese
9. Top with Greek yogurt and green onions

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 3 days refrigerated
- Substitutions: Pulled pork or beef
- Meal Prep: Bake potatoes ahead, reheat and assemble
