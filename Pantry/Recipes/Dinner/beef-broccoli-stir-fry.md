# Quick Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 12 minutes
**Cook Time:** 12 minutes
**Total Time:** 24 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 520 kcal |
| Protein | 48g |
| Carbs | 28g |
| Fat | 24g |
| Fiber | 5g |
| Sodium | 880mg |

## Ingredients

- 400g flank steak, sliced thin against grain
- 3 cups broccoli florets
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- Sesame seeds for garnish

## Instructions

1. Toss sliced beef with 1 tbsp soy sauce and cornstarch
2. Mix remaining soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, brown sugar
3. Heat 1 tbsp oil in wok over high heat
4. Stir fry beef 2-3 minutes until browned, remove
5. Add remaining oil, stir fry broccoli 3 minutes
6. Add garlic and ginger, cook 30 seconds
7. Return beef, add sauce
8. Stir fry 2 minutes until sauce thickens
9. Serve over white rice, garnish with sesame seeds

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 3 days refrigerated
- Substitutions: Chicken breast or shrimp
- Meal Prep: Slice beef and prep sauce ahead
