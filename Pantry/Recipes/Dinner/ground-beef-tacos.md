# Ground Beef Tacos with Black Beans

**Servings:** 2 (4 tacos each)
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 15 minutes
**Total Time:** 25 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving - 4 tacos)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 495 kcal |
| Protein | 34g |
| Carbs | 48g |
| Fat | 18g |
| Fiber | 10g |

## Ingredients

### Meat
- 1 lb ground beef (lean)
- 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade blend)
- 1/4 cup water

### Shells & Toppings
- 8 small corn tortillas (or flour)
- 1 cup shredded lettuce
- 1 cup diced tomatoes
- 1/2 cup shredded cheese
- 1/4 cup sour cream
- 1/4 cup salsa
- Lime wedges

## Instructions

1. Brown ground beef in large skillet over medium-high heat, 5-6 minutes
2. Drain excess fat if needed
3. Add taco seasoning and water, stir to combine
4. Add black beans, reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes
5. Warm tortillas in dry skillet or microwave
6. Fill each tortilla with beef and bean mixture
7. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, and salsa
8. Squeeze fresh lime over top
9. Serve immediately

## Notes

- **Storage:** Keep meat mixture separate from toppings; stores 3-4 days
- **Taco Tuesday:** Double the meat mixture and freeze half
- **Substitutions:** Use ground turkey for leaner option
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
