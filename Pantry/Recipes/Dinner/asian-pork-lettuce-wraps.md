# Ground Pork Lettuce Wraps (Asian Style)

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 12 minutes
**Total Time:** 27 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 380 kcal |
| Protein | 30g |
| Carbs | 28g |
| Fat | 16g |
| Fiber | 4g |

## Ingredients

### Meat Filling
- 1 lb ground pork
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
- 1/2 cup water chestnuts, diced
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- 3 green onions, sliced

### Sauce
- 3 tbsp hoisin sauce
- 2 tbsp soy sauce (low sodium)
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp sesame oil

### Wraps
- 1 head butter lettuce, leaves separated
- Sesame seeds for garnish
- Extra green onions for garnish

## Instructions

1. Mix sauce ingredients in small bowl, set aside
2. Heat large skillet over medium-high heat
3. Add ground pork, break into small pieces
4. Cook until no longer pink, about 6-7 minutes
5. Add garlic and ginger, cook 30 seconds
6. Add water chestnuts and carrots, cook 2 minutes
7. Pour sauce over meat mixture, stir to coat
8. Add green onions, cook 1 minute more
9. Spoon meat mixture into butter lettuce cups
10. Garnish with sesame seeds and extra green onions

## Notes

- **Storage:** Keep meat separate from lettuce; stores 3-4 days
- **Substitutions:** Use ground chicken or turkey for leaner option
- **Make it Spicier:** Add 1 tsp chili garlic sauce or sriracha
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
