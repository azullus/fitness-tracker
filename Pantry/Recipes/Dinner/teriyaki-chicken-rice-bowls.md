# Teriyaki Chicken Rice Bowls

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 18 minutes
**Total Time:** 28 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 560 kcal |
| Protein | 46g |
| Carbs | 58g |
| Fat | 14g |
| Fiber | 3g |
| Sodium | 820mg |

## Ingredients

- 400g chicken thighs, boneless, cubed
- 1.5 cups white rice, cooked
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
- 1 tbsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water
- Steamed broccoli
- Sesame seeds and green onions for garnish

## Instructions

1. Cook white rice according to package directions
2. Mix soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, garlic, and ginger
3. Heat sesame oil in skillet over medium-high
4. Cook chicken 6-8 minutes until browned and cooked through
5. Pour teriyaki sauce over chicken
6. Mix cornstarch slurry, add to pan
7. Simmer 2-3 minutes until sauce thickens
8. Serve over rice with steamed broccoli
9. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 4 days refrigerated
- Substitutions: Chicken breast or salmon
- Meal Prep: Excellent batch cooking recipe
