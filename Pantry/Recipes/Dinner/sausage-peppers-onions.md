# Italian Sausage with Peppers & Onions

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 8 minutes
**Cook Time:** 20 minutes
**Total Time:** 28 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 540 kcal |
| Protein | 36g |
| Carbs | 18g |
| Fat | 36g |
| Fiber | 4g |
| Sodium | 1020mg |

## Ingredients

- 4 Italian sausage links (400g)
- 2 bell peppers (mixed colors), sliced
- 1 large onion, sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 cup chicken broth
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- Fresh basil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Optional: 2 whole grain hoagie rolls

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high
2. Brown sausages on all sides, about 6 minutes total
3. Remove sausages, slice into coins
4. Add peppers and onions to pan, season with salt, pepper
5. Saut√© 8-10 minutes until softened and charred
6. Add garlic and Italian seasoning, cook 1 minute
7. Add broth, scrape up brown bits
8. Return sausage coins, simmer 3 minutes
9. Serve as is or in hoagie rolls, garnish with basil

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 4 days refrigerated
- Substitutions: Chicken sausage for lower fat
- Meal Prep: Excellent batch cooking recipe
