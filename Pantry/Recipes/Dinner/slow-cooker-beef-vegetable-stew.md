# Slow Cooker Beef & Vegetable Stew

**Servings:** 2 (with generous leftovers)
**Prep Time:** 20 minutes
**Cook Time:** 6-8 hours (slow cooker)
**Total Time:** 6-8 hours

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 425 kcal |
| Protein | 38g |
| Carbs | 35g |
| Fat | 14g |
| Fiber | 6g |

## Ingredients

### Beef
- 1.5 lb stewing beef, cut into 1.5-inch cubes
- 2 tbsp flour (for dredging)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper

### Vegetables & Broth
- 4 large carrots, chopped into 1-inch pieces
- 3 celery stalks, chopped
- 2 cups baby potatoes, halved
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 cups beef broth
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 bay leaves
- 4-5 sprigs fresh thyme (or 1 tsp dried)
- 2 cloves garlic, minced

## Instructions

1. Pat beef dry and season with salt and pepper
2. Dredge beef lightly in flour
3. Heat olive oil in large skillet over high heat
4. Brown beef on all sides in batches (don't overcrowd)
5. Transfer browned beef to slow cooker
6. Add all vegetables to slow cooker
7. Whisk tomato paste into beef broth
8. Pour broth over beef and vegetables
9. Add bay leaves, thyme, and garlic
10. Cover and cook on LOW 6-8 hours or HIGH 3-4 hours
11. Beef should be fork-tender when done
12. Remove bay leaves and thyme stems before serving
13. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste

## Notes

- **Storage:** Keeps 4-5 days refrigerated; freezes well for 3 months
- **Thickening:** Mix 1 tbsp cornstarch with 2 tbsp water, stir in last 30 min
- **Substitutions:** Use beef chuck roast, cut into cubes
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
