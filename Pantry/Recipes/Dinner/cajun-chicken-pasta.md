# Cajun Chicken Pasta

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 20 minutes
**Total Time:** 30 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 620 kcal |
| Protein | 52g |
| Carbs | 48g |
| Fat | 24g |
| Fiber | 4g |
| Sodium | 780mg |

## Ingredients

- 400g chicken breast, sliced
- 200g penne pasta
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1/2 onion, sliced
- 2 tbsp Cajun seasoning
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup chicken broth
- 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Fresh parsley
- Salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta according to package, drain
2. Season chicken generously with Cajun seasoning
3. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high
4. Cook chicken 6-7 minutes until cooked, remove
5. Saut√© peppers and onion until soft (4 min)
6. Add cream and broth, simmer 3 minutes
7. Stir in parmesan until melted
8. Add pasta and chicken, toss to coat
9. Garnish with parsley and serve

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 3 days refrigerated (cream may separate)
- Substitutions: Shrimp instead of chicken
- Meal Prep: Cook components separately, combine when serving
