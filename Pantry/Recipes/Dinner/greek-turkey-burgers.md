# Greek Turkey Burgers

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 12 minutes
**Cook Time:** 12 minutes
**Total Time:** 24 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 540 kcal |
| Protein | 50g |
| Carbs | 32g |
| Fat | 24g |
| Fiber | 3g |
| Sodium | 720mg |

## Ingredients

- 450g ground turkey
- 60g feta cheese, crumbled
- 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 2 whole grain burger buns
- 1/4 cup tzatziki sauce
- Lettuce, tomato, red onion for topping
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix turkey, half the feta, dill, garlic, oregano, salt, pepper
2. Form into 2 large patties
3. Heat grill or skillet over medium-high
4. Cook patties 5-6 minutes per side until 165Â°F internal
5. Toast buns lightly
6. Spread tzatziki on bun bottoms
7. Add patty, remaining feta, lettuce, tomato, onion
8. Add bun tops and serve

## Notes

- Storage: Cooked patties keep 4 days
- Substitutions: Ground chicken or lean beef
- Meal Prep: Form patties ahead, cook fresh
