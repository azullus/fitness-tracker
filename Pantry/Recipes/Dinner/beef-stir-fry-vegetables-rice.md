# Beef Stir-Fry with Mixed Vegetables & Rice

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 15 minutes
**Total Time:** 30 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 485 kcal |
| Protein | 35g |
| Carbs | 48g |
| Fat | 16g |
| Fiber | 5g |

## Ingredients

### Stir-Fry
- 1 lb ground beef (lean)
- 3 cups mixed stir-fry vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas, carrots)
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced (optional)
- 2 tbsp soy sauce (low sodium)
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 green onions, sliced

### Rice
- 2 cups cooked brown rice

## Instructions

1. Cook brown rice according to package directions (or use pre-cooked)
2. Heat sesame oil in large wok or skillet over high heat
3. Add ground beef, break apart with spatula
4. Cook until browned, about 5-6 minutes
5. Add garlic and ginger, cook 30 seconds until fragrant
6. Add mixed vegetables, stir-fry 4-5 minutes until crisp-tender
7. Add soy sauce, toss to coat
8. Divide rice between two bowls
9. Top with beef and vegetable stir-fry
10. Garnish with sliced green onions

## Notes

- **Storage:** Keeps 3-4 days refrigerated; reheat in microwave or skillet
- **Substitutions:** Use sliced beef sirloin instead of ground for a different texture
- **Make it Spicier:** Add sriracha or red pepper flakes
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
