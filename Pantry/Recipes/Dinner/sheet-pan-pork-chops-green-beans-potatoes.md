# Sheet Pan Pork Chops with Green Beans & Potatoes

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 30 minutes
**Total Time:** 45 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 485 kcal |
| Protein | 40g |
| Carbs | 36g |
| Fat | 20g |
| Fiber | 6g |

## Ingredients

### Pork Chops
- 4 bone-in pork chops (about 1 lb total)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- Fresh rosemary, chopped
- Salt and pepper

### Vegetables
- 1 lb baby potatoes, halved
- 1 lb fresh green beans, trimmed
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)
2. Toss potatoes with 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper
3. Spread potatoes on sheet pan in single layer
4. Roast potatoes for 10 minutes
5. Meanwhile, pat pork chops dry
6. Mix paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and rosemary
7. Rub pork chops with 1 tbsp olive oil and seasoning blend
8. Toss green beans with remaining olive oil and garlic
9. After 10 minutes, flip potatoes
10. Add pork chops and green beans to pan
11. Roast additional 18-20 minutes until pork reaches 145°F
12. Let rest 5 minutes before serving

## Notes

- **Storage:** Keeps 3-4 days refrigerated
- **Even Cooking:** Cut potatoes same size for even roasting
- **Substitutions:** Use asparagus or broccoli instead of green beans
- **Banana Allergy:** This recipe is banana-free and safe for her
