# Mediterranean Chicken Skewers

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 12 minutes
**Total Time:** 27 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 460 kcal |
| Protein | 48g |
| Carbs | 12g |
| Fat | 24g |
| Fiber | 3g |
| Sodium | 580mg |

## Ingredients

- 400g chicken breast, cubed
- 1 zucchini, sliced thick
- 1 red bell pepper, chunked
- 1 red onion, chunked
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp paprika
- Tzatziki for serving
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. If using wooden skewers, soak 15 minutes
2. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, garlic powder, paprika, salt, pepper
3. Toss chicken and vegetables in marinade
4. Thread onto skewers, alternating chicken and veggies
5. Heat grill or grill pan to medium-high
6. Grill skewers 5-6 minutes per side until chicken is cooked
7. Let rest 3 minutes
8. Serve with tzatziki sauce

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 3 days refrigerated
- Substitutions: Shrimp or lamb cubes
- Meal Prep: Prep skewers ahead, grill fresh
