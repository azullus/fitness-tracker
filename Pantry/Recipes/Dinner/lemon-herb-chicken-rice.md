# Lemon Herb Chicken with White Rice

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 20 minutes
**Total Time:** 30 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 540 kcal |
| Protein | 48g |
| Carbs | 45g |
| Fat | 16g |
| Fiber | 2g |
| Sodium | 520mg |

## Ingredients

- 2 chicken breasts (400g total)
- 1.5 cups white rice, cooked
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 lemon, juiced and zested
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1/4 cup chicken broth
- Fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Pound chicken breasts to even thickness
2. Season with salt, pepper, oregano, and thyme
3. Heat oil in skillet over medium-high
4. Cook chicken 5-6 minutes per side until golden and cooked through
5. Remove chicken, rest 5 minutes
6. In same pan, add garlic, cook 30 seconds
7. Add lemon juice, zest, and broth, scrape up brown bits
8. Slice chicken, serve over rice with pan sauce
9. Garnish with fresh parsley

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 4 days refrigerated
- Substitutions: Chicken thighs for juicier meat
- Meal Prep: Great for batch cooking
