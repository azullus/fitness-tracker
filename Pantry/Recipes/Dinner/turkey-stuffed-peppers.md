# Turkey Stuffed Bell Peppers

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 12 minutes
**Cook Time:** 25 minutes
**Total Time:** 37 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 520 kcal |
| Protein | 48g |
| Carbs | 35g |
| Fat | 22g |
| Fiber | 6g |
| Sodium | 680mg |

## Ingredients

- 450g ground turkey
- 4 bell peppers, tops cut, seeded
- 1 cup white rice, cooked
- 1 can diced tomatoes
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1/2 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp chili powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
2. Brown turkey with onion and garlic
3. Add cumin, chili powder, salt, pepper
4. Stir in cooked rice and half the diced tomatoes
5. Stand peppers in baking dish
6. Fill peppers with turkey mixture
7. Pour remaining tomatoes around peppers
8. Cover with foil, bake 20 minutes
9. Remove foil, top with cheese, bake 5 more minutes

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 4 days refrigerated
- Substitutions: Ground beef or chicken
- Meal Prep: Stuff peppers ahead, bake when ready
