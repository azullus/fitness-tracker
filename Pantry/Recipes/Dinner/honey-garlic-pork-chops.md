# Honey Garlic Pork Chops

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 8 minutes
**Cook Time:** 18 minutes
**Total Time:** 26 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 480 kcal |
| Protein | 45g |
| Carbs | 22g |
| Fat | 22g |
| Fiber | 1g |
| Sodium | 580mg |

## Ingredients

- 2 bone-in pork chops (400g total)
- 3 tbsp honey
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- Fresh thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Season pork chops with salt and pepper
2. Heat oil in skillet over medium-high
3. Sear pork chops 4-5 minutes per side until golden
4. Remove pork, set aside
5. Reduce heat, add butter and garlic, cook 30 seconds
6. Add honey, soy sauce, and mustard, stir to combine
7. Return pork to pan, spoon sauce over
8. Simmer 3-4 minutes, basting frequently
9. Garnish with fresh thyme

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 3 days refrigerated
- Substitutions: Boneless chops (reduce cook time)
- Meal Prep: Make sauce ahead, cook pork fresh
