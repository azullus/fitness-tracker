# One-Pan Salmon & Asparagus

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 8 minutes
**Cook Time:** 15 minutes
**Total Time:** 23 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 480 kcal |
| Protein | 46g |
| Carbs | 12g |
| Fat | 28g |
| Fiber | 5g |
| Sodium | 480mg |

## Ingredients

- 2 salmon fillets (350g total)
- 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 lemon, sliced
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- Fresh dill
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C)
2. Line baking sheet with parchment
3. Toss asparagus with 1 tbsp oil, salt, pepper, spread on pan
4. Season salmon with paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper
5. Place salmon on pan with asparagus
6. Drizzle remaining oil, add minced garlic and lemon slices
7. Bake 12-15 minutes until salmon flakes easily
8. Garnish with fresh dill and serve

## Notes

- Storage: Best eaten fresh, salmon dries out reheated
- Substitutions: Broccoli or green beans instead of asparagus
- Meal Prep: Not recommended
