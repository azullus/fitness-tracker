# Balsamic Glazed Chicken

**Servings:** 2
**Prep Time:** 8 minutes
**Cook Time:** 20 minutes
**Total Time:** 28 minutes

## Nutrition Facts (per serving)

| Nutrient | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Calories | 440 kcal |
| Protein | 48g |
| Carbs | 18g |
| Fat | 18g |
| Fiber | 2g |
| Sodium | 520mg |

## Ingredients

- 2 chicken breasts (400g total)
- 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
- 2 tbsp honey
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- Fresh basil leaves
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, Italian seasoning
2. Heat oil in oven-safe skillet over medium-high
3. Sear chicken 4 minutes per side until golden
4. Add garlic, cook 30 seconds
5. Add balsamic vinegar and honey, stir
6. Add cherry tomatoes around chicken
7. Transfer to oven at 400°F for 8-10 minutes until chicken reaches 165°F
8. Let rest 5 minutes
9. Garnish with fresh basil, spoon glaze over chicken

## Notes

- Storage: Keeps 4 days refrigerated
- Substitutions: Chicken thighs for juicier meat
- Meal Prep: Great for batch cooking
